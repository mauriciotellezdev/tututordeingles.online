A couple of strategic changes I would make beyond the prompt:

1. **The QR code should not go to the homepage.**  
   It should go directly to `/registro` because someone scanning a flyer is already interested. Don't make them hunt.
2. **Your first goal is not 100 students.**  
   Your first milestone is:

- 1 beginner group (6)
- 1 intermediate group (6)
- 1 teen group (6)

18 students × 800 = **14,400 MXN/month**

That already nearly doubles your wife's current income while only requiring ~4–5 teaching hours/week.

3. The website should sell the **experience**, not the English. The English is the outcome. The product is belonging to a small group where people finally feel comfortable speaking.
